What we do with your cacao.

What does your cacao go through with us? First, your endorsement catches our attention! To make sure you are indeed producing the cacaos endorsed, you go through our extensive authentication process, which involves creating an immutable and tamperproof record of transaction.

We then tokenize the cacaos you deliver! A special QR code will be placed on each of your cacaos you produced. These QR codes will contain the origin, quality, authenticity, and the price to be dictated by yours alone.

Your cacaos are then tracked on the way to the shelves and tables, ensuring reliable transactions, harvests, packaging and shipping are recorded in our special QR code. These cacaos are then verified by the blockchain token located in QR code as well as in our records!

Finally, your best quality cacaos are then sold to the leading marketplaces and consumers. Once sold, the tamperproof QR code which holds the one-of-a-kind token that held the records of where your cacao has gone through, will have an ending at the last of the list with the name of the first customer. This ensures that after leaving the factories, your cacaos have first arrived safely to a loyal customer. A reliable and protected way of selling your cacaos straight to the customer with authenticity!
